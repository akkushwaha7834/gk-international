Manufacturing Excellence at GK International

At GK International, we pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities that set new standards in the paper bag industry. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability is reflected in every aspect of our production process.

Our manufacturing excellence is the cornerstone of our success in the global paper bag market. We continuously invest in upgrading our facilities and processes to meet the evolving needs of our international clients, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of sustainable packaging solutions.

Paper Bag Manufacturing Process

  • Primary raw material is wood pulp derived from trees
  • Materials include paper fibers, wood pulp, recycled paper
  • Additional components: bleaching agents, fillers, starch sizing agents
  • Dyes and pigments used for coloration
  • Wood chips or paper soaked in water to create pulp slurry
  • Chemicals used to clean and purify pulp
  • Impurities removed during the washing process
  • The bleaching process improves whiteness and paper quality
  • Pulp spread onto wire mesh conveyor belt
  • Water content drained during movement
  • Continuous paper sheet created
  • Determines the bag's durability and appearance
  • Pattern cut from printed sheets
  • Precise machine folding ensures uniform shape
  • Seams glued using eco-friendly adhesives
  • Bottom reinforced with extra paper or cardboard layers

Bag Formation

  • Paper tube formation defines bag size
  • Hydraulic systems mount paper rolls
  • Tension control units maintain paper web alignment
  • Cutting machines trim tubes to the required length

Final Processing

  • Handles attached using staples or strong adhesives
  • Bags collected in batches (typically 50 bags)
  • Packaged in corrugated material to prevent damage
  • Quality inspection ensures product meets standards
